# KISS Audio - User Manual

Welcome to **KISS Audio**, your all-in-one Android center for Music, Radio, and Podcasts. Built on the **Keep It Simple, Stupid** principle, this app is designed to be a reliable tool that respects your time and your focus.

## 1. Core Concept: Context-Specific States
Unlike standard players, KISS Audio is designed around **Listening States**. 
- A **Music Deck** for the Gym remembers your high-energy playlist and exactly where you left off.
- A **Radio Station** for the Morning News is one tap away.
- A **Podcast** for your commute keeps track of your downloaded episodes.

The app remembers your position in every category independently. You can switch from a podcast to a radio station and back again without ever losing your spot.

---

## 2. Music Decks
Use Music Decks to manage different folders on your phone.

### Setting up a Deck
1.  Navigate to the **Music** tab.
2.  Tap **Add New Music Deck**.
3.  Give it a name (e.g., "Chill Mix").
4.  Tap **Select Folder** on the Deck card.
5.  Navigate to the folder on your phone and tap **Use this folder**.
6.  The app will scan for MP3 files and prepare your deck.

### Using Decks
- **Switching:** Simply tap another deck to switch contexts. The app remembers your position in every deck independently.
- **Controls:** Supports Shuffle, Repeat, and standard transport controls. Metadata (Title, Artist, Album) is automatically displayed if available in the files.
- **Change Folder:** Tap "Change" to select a new directory. The app will remember your last location.

---

## 3. Radio Stations
Listen to live streams from around the world.

### Finding Stations
1.  Navigate to the **Radio** tab.
2.  Tap the **Search (Magnifying Glass)** icon in the top bar.
3.  Type the name of a station or country.
4.  Tap a result to add it to your dashboard.

### Manual Addition
1.  Tap **Add New Station** at the bottom of the list.
2.  Enter the Station Name and the Stream URL (http/https).

---

## 4. Podcasts
Subscribe to your favorite shows and listen offline.

### Subscribing
1.  Navigate to the **Podcasts** tab.
2.  Ensure you are on the **Shows** tab.
3.  Tap the **Search (Magnifying Glass)** icon in the top bar to search for any podcast by name.
4.  Alternatively, tap **Subscribe to RSS** to manually paste a feed URL.

### Managing Episodes
- **Shows View:** See all your subscribed podcasts. Tap one to see its full episode list.
- **Recent View:** A unified list of all recently published episodes from all your subscriptions.
- **Downloading:** Tap the **Download** icon on an episode to save it for offline listening. 
- **Streaming:** Tap **Play/Stream** to start listening immediately without downloading.

### Smart Features
- **Auto-Cleanup:** Once you finish an episode (or have less than 30 seconds left), the app automatically marks it as played and deletes the downloaded file to save space.
- **Hide Played:** Toggle the **Visibility** icon in the top bar to hide/show episodes you've already finished.

---

## 5. Global Controls
- **Mini-Player:** At the bottom of every screen, a mini-player shows what's currently playing. Tap it to navigate to the detailed player (or episode detail).
- **Bottom Navigation:** Quickly switch between Music, Radio, and Podcasts using the icons at the bottom.

---

## 6. Home Hub Mode (Launcher)
KISS Audio can act as your device's primary interface, turning a tablet or old phone into a dedicated music station.

### Setting as Default Launcher
1.  Open your device's **Settings**.
2.  Navigate to **Apps** -> **Default Apps**.
3.  Tap **Home app**.
4.  Select **KISS Audio**.

### Using Hub Mode
- **Stay Focused:** Pressing the physical or virtual Home button will now always bring you back to your audio controls.
- **System Access:** A **Clock** icon in the top bar jumps to your system alarms and time settings, making it easy to use the device as a bedside alarm clock.
- **Edge-to-Edge:** The app automatically uses every pixel of your screen for a clean, immersive look.
