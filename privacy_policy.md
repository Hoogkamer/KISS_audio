# Privacy Policy for KISS Audio

**Last Updated: May 9, 2026**

## 1. Overview
KISS Audio ("the App") is designed with a "Privacy First" approach. We believe your listening habits should remain private. The App is a local-first audio player and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data to our servers.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
- **Personal Information**: We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.
- **Audio Files**: The App accesses local storage to play your music files. This data never leaves your device.
- **Radio & Podcasts**: When you stream radio or podcasts, the App connects directly to the provider's server. Your IP address may be visible to those providers as part of the standard HTTP request, but the App does not track or store these interactions.
- **Analytics**: The App does NOT use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

## 3. Permissions
- **Notifications**: Used to show playback controls.
- **Storage/Media Access**: Used to scan and play audio files from your device.
- **Internet**: Used only for streaming radio and downloading podcast episodes.
- **Launcher Mode**: Used to provide a dedicated home screen for your device (KISS Audio as the primary interface).

## 4. Data Security
Since no data is collected or stored on our servers, there is no risk of your data being compromised from our side. We recommend using device-level security (PIN/Biometrics) to protect the files on your phone.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository.
